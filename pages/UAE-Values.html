Traditions and Customs

The UAE is known for its rich traditions and customs, including hospitality, falconry, and traditional dances like Al-Ayyala.

Cultural Heritage Sites

Explore the UAE's cultural heritage sites such as Al Fahidi Historical Neighborhood, Al Ain Oasis, and the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.

Founding of the UAE

The UAE was founded on December 2, 1971, by the union of seven emirates. Key figures include Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan, the first President of the UAE.

Recommended books: "Zayed: Man Who Built a Nation" and "From Rags to Riches: A Story of Abu Dhabi".

National Identity

National identity in the UAE is deeply rooted in its history, culture, and traditions. It encompasses the values of unity, pride, and respect for heritage.

Key elements include the Arabic language, traditional dress, and the celebration of national events such as National Day and Flag Day.

About the UAE

The United Arab Emirates (UAE) is a federal state composed of seven emirates, with a constitution and full sovereignty. Its capital is Abu Dhabi. This section provides information about the country's economy, political system, government, plans, strategies, initiatives, history, future, and culture. Year of Sustainability

2023-2024 - Year of Sustainability. The essence of sustainability is protecting our land, resources, and each other. It is a comprehensive commitment in the UAE to continue preserving our environment and sustainability legacy for a better tomorrow.

We the UAE 2031 Vision

The "We the UAE 2031" vision aims to increase the country's GDP from AED 1.49 trillion to AED 3 trillion. The vision represents a national action plan to continue the UAE's developmental journey for the next decade, focusing on social, economic, investment, and developmental aspects.

UAE Visual Media Identity

The UAE government, with local and global public participation, adopted the "Seven Lines" design for the UAE's visual media identity, representing the seven emirates and seven founders in the global development race, to be used with the slogan "Impossible is Possible" in all strategic sectors and events that convey the UAE's story to the world.

UAE Digital Government

His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum launched the Smart Government initiative in 2013 to provide services to the public 24/7, a government that reaches citizens at all times and places worldwide. The initiative aligns with the UAE government's direction to develop government services and achieve a high quality of life for citizens and residents according to the UAE Vision 2021.

UAE Centennial 2071

The "UAE Centennial 2071" is a long-term government work program that includes developing a national strategy to enhance the country's reputation and soft power, ensuring diverse sources of government revenue away from oil, investing in education, building an Emirati ethical value system in future generations, raising productivity in the national economy, and enhancing social cohesion.
